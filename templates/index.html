Genre and Objective

Teen Trailblazer is an educational simulation game designed to empower teenagers aged 14 to 18 to explore various career paths and ignite their passions. Through engaging scenarios, players can discover their interests and potential, aiding informed decisions about their futures.

Narrative and Gameplay

In 'Teen Trailblazer,' players embark on a journey of self-discovery... Each selection provides an overview of the role, highlighting its benefits and challenges.

As players progress, they encounter quests tailored to their chosen career... The outcomes of these decisions shape the player's journey, offering a dynamic and engaging experience that encourages exploration and self-reflection.
